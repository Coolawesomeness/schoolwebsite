Campus Life

school church

Was established in 1978

The school church is where the students who pray to a certain god come to pray. We have many statues of different gods within the church.

school library

Was built in 1980

The school library is where all grades read and checkout books. We also make sure we have all the books that are recommended by students studying in our school.

School Gym

Was built in 1982

The school gym is where the sixth, seventh, and eighth graders exersise. They also play games such as basketball or dodgeball.

school achievements

Inter School Debate On Environment Pollution
Won first prize in inter school debate on environment pollution

It was a fascinating moment for the students who participated in the debate. They voted on ways to stop environmental pollution. Whoever had the most clever idea to clean up the environment would get a prize.

Inter School Football Competition
Came first in inter school football competition

The students who loved to play football looked at this as an opportunity to show the school how talented they were. Students trained every day to get ready for the competition. When the contest was over, the coaches decided to pick ten people to join the team. The best one to get elected on the team was able to get a prize.

Inter School Science Project Competition
Won first prize in inter school Science Project competition

Students who wanted to get many prizes and were very talented were able to participate in the competition. Many decided to join the event, but many were not capable of winning the tournament. Finally, a person was chosen to get first prize.

What's New

  • Hybrid Learning

  • Hybrid learning is beneficial for the people who want to have more experience of going to school. Ever since the pandemic was there, most of the students had to have school online. Now, limited people can go to school and get the full experience of learning, without getting sick. One of the biggest reasons kids need to go to school is to socialize and talk to their friends. Hybrid learning lets kids socialize without getting sick. Parents also believe that younger kids can't sit on the screen for the whole day. Kids can now have fun and not get sick at the same time. An article online said that most of the kids in the United States hate the pandemic. They hate that they can't hang out with their friends and they also hate that they can't go outside. School is one of the only ways kids can hang out with their friends.

  • Plans to Expand School

    A proposal is set to come before city officals to discuss the expansion of Granger Middle School. The plan would increase the size of the campus and accomodate an extra 220 students. The new building would be used by students during the day but would also be available to the community school hours."This new center would both expand the school's population and give a new recreational center for people in our community. It would be a great addition to District 204 students," said Mr.Davenport. Although the new plan would allow more people to send their kids to a school closer to home, some think that it would pose more problems for the surrounding area.
